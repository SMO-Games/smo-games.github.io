How to Play

Guessing

Guess any runner name to begin.

You will be shown a set of 6 statistics for the runner you guessed,
and how they compare to the correct runner.

You can hover over the ? symbols to read what each one means.

You have 7 total guesses to use the information provided
to figure out who the correct runner is.

Good luck!


Extra Info

Who is included?

Currently, the Top 150 Runners in Any% 1P are all included.

How often is the data updated?

Every six hours.

How often does the game reset?

Once per day.
